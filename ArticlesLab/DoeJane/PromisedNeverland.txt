The promised neverland is a 2019 anime adaptation of the manga of the same name.
It follows a group of children attempting to escape an orphanage that is raising them to be food for  monsters.
It is a thriller, with each episode revealing a new challenge for the children to overcome and raising the danger of them being caught.
The main characters are Emma: the physically strongest of the children and deeply caring.
Norman: considered the brains of the orphans, he devises much of their escape plan.
And Ray who is cunning and constantly one step ahead of the adults trying to stop them.
